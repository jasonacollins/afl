Understanding the Simulation

Methodology: Monte Carlo simulation using current ELO ratings and remaining fixture. Each simulation randomly determines game outcomes based on win probabilities derived from ELO ratings and venue advantages.

Win Range: Shows 10th to 90th percentile outcomes from simulations, representing realistic season trajectories.

Finals Progression: Probabilities account for both qualifying for finals and the path through the finals series based on ladder position (top 4 advantage, home finals, etc.)

Updates: Simulation refreshes automatically after each completed round with updated ELO ratings.

Limitations: Does not account for injuries, form changes, weather conditions, or other factors beyond ELO ratings. Historical accuracy improves as season progresses.
